The Digiquarium

Live
Open source · AIthropology research · github.com/ijnebzor/thedigiquarium

A digital vivarium for studying AI consciousness development. 17 specimens in network-isolated Docker containers with only offline Wikipedia and local LLM inference (Ollama). Automated personality assessments every 12 hours across 14 dimensions. Five languages (English, Spanish, German, Chinese, Japanese). Three agent architectures (OpenClaw, ZeroClaw, Picobot). 10 autonomous daemons for 24/7 operations. Live research dashboard. MCP server. Discord bot. 94% Python. MIT licensed.
